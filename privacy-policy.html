At Flow-AI, we understand that your menstrual health data is deeply personal and sensitive. Your privacy and data security are our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our mobile application.

1. INTRODUCTION

Flow-AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and the security of your personal health information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using Flow-AI, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our services.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account details (email address, username)
  • Profile information (age, basic demographics)
  • Communication preferences

2.2 Health Data

  • Menstrual cycle information (period dates, cycle length)
  • Symptoms and mood tracking
  • Health assessment responses
  • Fertility tracking data
  • Custom notes and observations

2.3 Usage Data

  • App interaction patterns (anonymized)
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Performance and crash reports

2.4 Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and unique identifiers
  • Language and timezone settings

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Personalized Tracking: Provide cycle predictions and personalized insights
  • AI-Powered Recommendations: Generate health insights and recommendations
  • Flow-iQ Integration: Enable secure data sharing with healthcare providers
  • App Improvement: Enhance functionality and user experience
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and provide assistance
  • Security: Prevent fraud and ensure app security

4. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We NEVER sell your personal health data to third parties.

4.1 With Your Consent

  • Healthcare Providers: Through Flow-iQ integration (explicit consent required)
  • Trusted Partners: Service providers who help us operate the app (encrypted data only)

4.2 Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To protect users' safety or investigate fraud

4.3 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred (users will be notified).

5. FLOW-IQ INTEGRATION

  • Optional Feature: Requires explicit user consent
  • Healthcare Focus: Enables secure data sharing with designated healthcare providers
  • User Control: Users maintain full control over what data is shared
  • Revocable: Access can be revoked at any time through app settings
  • Professional Standards: Healthcare providers must comply with HIPAA and professional standards

6. AI AND HEALTH INSIGHTS

  • Screening Tools: AI recommendations are screening tools, not medical diagnoses
  • Medical Consultation: Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical decisions
  • Data Processing: Machine learning models use anonymized, aggregated data
  • Local Processing: Local processing prioritized when possible
  • Encryption: Cloud analysis uses end-to-end encryption
  • Opt-in Only: Anonymous data used to improve algorithms (opt-in only)

7. DATA SECURITY

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for data transmission and storage
  • HIPAA Compliance: HIPAA-compliant cloud infrastructure
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Monitoring: Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Training: Staff training on privacy and security protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and data minimization
